Lead Story

Ojuola & Efe Mark Eight Remarkable Years

In what family and friends are calling "an extraordinary union," the couple celebrates a milestone that has come to define a generation of love, patience, and quiet devotion — right here in Moncton.

By The Family  ·  Special Correspondent

Ojuola and Efe — eight years and still the most beautiful thing in the room. — Family Archive

Eight years ago, two people found each other and quietly decided that the world made more sense together than apart. What followed was not the dramatic, headline-grabbing kind of love — it was something far more rare. It was the kind of love you build with your hands: steady, deliberate, and deepened by every season that passes.

From their traditional wedding day — surrounded by colour, family, and the kind of joy that only comes from knowing you have made the right choice — to the life they have since built in Moncton, Ojuola and Efe have never stopped growing toward each other. They brought two beautiful boys, Zane and Zuriel, into the world, and in doing so, turned a love story into a family legacy.

Those who know them well speak of this couple with a particular kind of reverence. "They just make sense together," one family member noted.

Eight years. Not a milestone to simply celebrate — one to study, to learn from, and to honour with the full weight of family love behind it. This edition is that honour.

The Day It Became Official

Surrounded by family, colour, and a joy that filled every corner of the room, Ojuola and Efe said yes — the traditional way, with the full blessing of those who love them most. This was the day a love story became a covenant, and everyone present knew exactly what they were witnessing.

The early days — just the two of them, figuring it all out.

Early Years

Before It Was Easy

Every great love has its early chapter — the one before the routines settle in, before everything has a name. For Ojuola and Efe, this was the time of discovery: learning each other's silences, building their language, choosing to stay when staying was still a decision and not yet a reflex.

Then there were four — Zane, Zuriel, and a family made whole.

The Family Years

Zane, Zuriel & Everything Changed

When uriel arrived, and then Zayne, the love between Ojuola and Efe did not divide — it multiplied. They did not just build a marriage. They built a home, a team, and two young lives that carry both of them in every smile.

Moncton, 2026 — the family, eight years strong.

Today — Moncton, Canada

Eight Years On, Still Growing

Today, far from where it all began but never far from the people who love them, Ojuola and Efe stand as proof that love is not a feeling you fall into — it is a decision you wake up and make, over and over, until it becomes the shape of your life. Eight years of that decision has built something remarkable: a partnership, a family, a story the whole family is proud to be part of.

Zane and Zuriel will grow up knowing what real love looks like — because they live inside it every single day. That is the greatest thing Ojuola and Efe have done. Not just for each other, but for all of us.
